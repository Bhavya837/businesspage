Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkillConnect?

SKillConnect is a skill-sharing platform where users can exchange knowledge and expertise in a fee-free environment through a barter system.


How does the barter system work??

Instead of paying for courses, learners can offer their own skills in return for enrolling in a class. Verified instructors and learners exchange skills based on mutual agreement.


Who can become an instructor?

Anyone with valuable knowledge or expertise in a subject can sign up as an instructor. We verify instructors based on their qualifications and feedback from learners.


Are there any fees for signing up?

No, our platform operates entirely on a no-fee basis. You can join as an instructor or learner without any monetary cost.
